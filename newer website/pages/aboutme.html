Alexis Ortiz is a Multimedia Artist; Growing up she always had an interest in different forms of art. Just like every young girl, her dream was to be a fashion designer until she discovered her passion for graphic design in middle school. She chose to continue to major in Illustration and Graphic Design at the high school of fashion industries. She also had an interest in photography and videography. During this time, she won 3rd place at the 7th annual city-wide graphic arts competition in 2008 for desktop publishing and design. She was the first middle school student to go up against high school students. She also won best perfume bottle design for the fragrance foundation in 2011.

Despite Having a deep love for Graphic Design, Alexis has decided to move on to other forms of art to expand her knowledge in digital media. She has grown appreciation of the art form and tries to establish her own adaptions from great artist who inspires her.
